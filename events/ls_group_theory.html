Learners' Space - Group Theory

This course dives into the fundamental structures that underpin modern mathematics, revealing the beauty and power of groups in solving complex problems.

This four-week course explores the fundamental concepts of number theory using algebraic techniques, with a focus on group theory. We will delve into the properties and applications of groups, subgroups, and cosets, and explore their connections to number theory.
